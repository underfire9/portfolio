Compass.app, Released

Dec201031

Happy new year 2011 from Handlino!

It has been a great year of 2010 for Handlino, but that does not stop us from pushing ourself forward. We have been recently working on a Ruby desktop application and it is ready just on time.

Yes, our first desktop product is proudly out: Compass.app.

Compass.app is a quick tool for designers to write Compass stylesheets (an enhanced CSS) easily without resorting to its command line interface. It’s cross-platform and runs on Mac, Windows, and even Linux.

Rapid prototyping is much easier with Compass and Compass.app then use plain-old CSS. We highly encourge you to familiarize with Compass because it actually makes fast prototyping fun, and productive.

Please visit Compass.app website at compass.handlion.com to take a look, and be sure to get it with our special new year offer of only $7 dollars price.

About

KKStream, initiated as KKBOX Video Department, spin off in Mar 2016 after two-year successful service development. We leverage technologies inherited from KKBOX Group with market-leading OTT (Over-the-top) solution to provide cloud-based media streaming service platform.

Product Introduction

KKStream's first service, Video Pass, launched in July 2015 Japan, as the next-generation (2.0) OTT service. Video Pass features a couple of thousand premium movies in variety of genres and catalogs. It provides rich business logics including FVOD, TVOD and SVOD with different user subscription plans.

The platform meets up-to-date standards: adaptive streaming as MPEG-DASH, HLS and MSS; industry-certificated content protection technology (DRM) as PlayReady, Widevine and FairPlay; multi-platform media players for Android smartphone, tablet, iPhone, iPad, set-top box (STB), smart TV and even Chromecast and AirPlay; scalable and self-hosted content encoding system for dynamic encoding workflows; both live and VOD adaptive video streaming with content usage analysis. The associated content management tools as a set of customizable tools to manage your content is also provided.

Recently, we are working on next-generation platform, featuring unified content design (live streaming, linear TV and VOD are all treated as first-class content), richer adaptive media players with social functions, and deeper viewer-centric content analysis sub-system.
